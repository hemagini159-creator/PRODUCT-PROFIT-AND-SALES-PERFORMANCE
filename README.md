# 📊 Product Profit and Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Product Profit and Sales Performance Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze and monitor overall business performance.
<img width="1563" height="738" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8a06cd3d-04d4-4706-8018-3142fc7acb3f" />

This dashboard provides detailed insights into:

* Sales trends
* Profit generation
* Discount impact
* Regional performance (Country & City level)
* Product-level performance

By transforming raw transactional data into meaningful visual insights, this project helps stakeholders make **data-driven decisions**, optimize pricing strategies, and improve overall profitability.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

* To analyze total sales, profit, and profit margin
* To track monthly and yearly sales performance trends
* To identify top-performing and low-performing products
* To evaluate country-wise and city-wise sales distribution
* To analyze the impact of discounts on profitability
* To support strategic business decision-making using data insights

---

## 🗂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional sales data with the following fields:

* Order Date
* Product Name
* Country
* City
* Sales
* Profit
* Discount
* Quantity
* Cost of Sales
* Price

📅 **Time Period Covered:** 2016–2019
📦 Includes product-level and region-level sales performance data.

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### 1️⃣ Sales & Profit by Month

* Displays monthly sales and profit trends
* Identifies seasonal fluctuations
* Helps track consistency in performance

---

### 2️⃣ Sales & Profit by Year

* Compares yearly growth
* Highlights business expansion trends
* Shows long-term performance patterns

---

### 3️⃣ Product Performance Analysis

* Identifies top-selling products
* Highlights high-revenue product categories
* Detects low-performing products

---

### 4️⃣ Country-Level Analysis

* Compares sales distribution across countries
* Analyzes discount trends by region
* Evaluates regional contribution to revenue

---

### 5️⃣ City-Level Analysis

* Identifies high-revenue cities
* Highlights key urban markets
* Supports targeted marketing strategies

---

### 6️⃣ Interactive Filters

* Country slicer for dynamic filtering
* Enables customized and user-driven analysis
* Improves dashboard interactivity

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard creation and visualization
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures and KPIs
* **Excel / CSV Dataset** – Data source
* **Data Modeling** – Relationship building and data transformation

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

* Sales show consistent growth across years
* A small group of products contributes significantly to total revenue
* Urban cities generate the highest sales volume
* Profit margin remains strong despite discount offerings
* Discount strategy is controlled and does not heavily impact profitability

---

## ✅ Conclusion

The **Product Profit and Sales Performance Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of business performance through interactive visual analytics.

By integrating sales trends, profitability metrics, and regional insights, this project demonstrates how Business Intelligence tools like Power BI can convert raw data into actionable business insights.
